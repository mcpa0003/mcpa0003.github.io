Cloudiness

Cloudiness Map

It seems that latitude does not play much of a role on the cloudiness of a city. A quick glance at the scatter plot which compares the amount of cloud cover against the latitude on which a city is located reveals that all latitudes can be very cloudy, somewhat cloudy, or not cloudy at all. Out of all the data on this particular plot, the only other trend that could possibly be pointed out might be that cities located above sixty degrees north latitude have a greater chance of being more than 50% cloudy than less than 50% cloudy.
