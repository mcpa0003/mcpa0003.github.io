Max Temperature

Temperature Map

Is it true that the closer you get to the equator, the warmer the temperature? The data gathered in this study seems to confirm just that! Of course, we need to take into consideration that when the data was gathered it was relatively close to equinox, which means that the earth’s rotation was on an axis that placed the equator nearly perpendicular to the sun. An analysis of the scatter plot graph reveals a general trend line of “the closer to the equator, the warmer the temperature.”
