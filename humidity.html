Humidity

Humidity Map

The data in this study seems to show that there are certain latitudes where lower than 50% humidity is not likely. Of course, this could simply mean that there are few population centers in places where low humidity is the norm at certain latitudes. Due to the conflicting nature of this data (one might think there is a general trend, “the hotter the temperature, the drier the climate”.) there is some information here about the reason deserts are not located near the equator.
